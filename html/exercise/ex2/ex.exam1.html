A scale-down model of the acoustic wave-mediated cell retention technology was used in combination with a mini-bioreactor system to develop an intensified perfusion process for the production of recombinant lentiviruses. The enhanced process was scaled up to bench-top bioreactor scale, resulting in a threefold increase in functional vector yield.
